What's #OpenAudubon?

The COVID-19 crisis opened the Audubon Park golf course to the public. We throw frisbees, have picnics, and nap on land that's usually reserved for golfing.

#OpenAudubon archives our memories during this historic time.

Everytime someone adds #OpenAudubon to an Instagram post, the post's image and captions are saved to our servers.

Pretty soon, you will be able to browse our archive on this site, which we eventually hope to donate to the Historic New Orleans Collection.

Want to help?

This is a volunteer run project, help us!..

  1. Tag instagram posts #OpenAudubon. We're particularly looking for posts that celebrate fun and inspiration found on the Audubon Park's golf course.
  2. Get out the word! Tell your friends about #OpenAudubon. The larger the record, the more fun!
  3. Help us code. The #OpenAudubon project is an open source project. Checkout our GitHub.
  4. Donate. We would love to print photos and pay for internet hosting. If you can, click here to donate.
