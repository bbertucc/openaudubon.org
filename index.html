Project Info

What's #OpenAudubon?

The COVID-19 crisis opened the Audubon Park golf course to the public. We throw frisbees, have picnics by the lake, and nap under ancient oak trees.

#OpenAudubon archives our memories during this historic time. Everytime someone adds #OpenAudobn to their Instagram post, their post's image and captions are saved to our servers. You can browse our archive on this site, which we eventually hope to donate to the Historic New Orleans Collection.

Want to help?

This is a volunteer run project, you can help us by:
  1. Tagging instagram posts #OpenAudubon. We're particularly looking for posts that celebrate fun and inspiration found on the Audubon Park Golf course.
  2. Get out the word! Tell your friends about #OpenAudubon. The larger the record, the more fun!
  3. Help us code. The #OpenAudubon project is an open source project. Checkout our GitHub.
  4. Donate. We would love to print photos and pay for internet hosting. If you can, click here to donate.

Contact Us!

Our email is info@openaudubon.org
